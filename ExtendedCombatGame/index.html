How the game works:

Select Characters:

The Warrior: baseDamage = 18, strongDamage = 22, armor = 9

The Mage: baseDamage = 7, strongDamage = 40, armor = 6

The Rogue: baseDamage = 16, strongDamage = 29, armor = 8

The Easy Enemy: baseDamage = 15, strongDamage = 20, armor = 5

The Hard Enemy: baseDamage = 18, strongDamage = 29, armor = 8

Click Attack Buttons:

Basic Attack will do baseDamage + d20 bonus

if you roll less than 5 on d20, you miss and take damage

if you roll greater than 15 on d20, you do bonus damage

It is possible to 'hit' for negative damage (heals the enemy)

Whenver you attack, the enemy attacks using the same mechanics.

Each attack uses stamina: damage must be >= stamina to use either attack

Armor is factored into damage but deteriorates as damage is taken

Heal will usually give your character more health, and will always reset stamina to 100

Whoever puts the opponents health at or below 0 wins
